Welcome to FraudShield

We leverage data-driven techniques to detect and prevent fraudulent card transactions in real-time.

How We Detect Fraud

Our detection system analyzes key transaction features using intelligent algorithms to identify suspicious activity. Here are the main fields we use:

  • Distance from Home: Checks if the transaction location is unusually far from the cardholder’s registered address.
  • Distance from Last Transaction: Measures how far this transaction is from the previous one, to detect unlikely travel patterns.
  • Ratio to Median Purchase Price: Compares transaction amount to the cardholder's typical spending habits.
  • Repeat Retailer: Indicates if the merchant is one the cardholder has used before.
  • Used Chip: Validates whether the chip on the card was used, increasing transaction security.
  • Used PIN Number: Confirms if the user authenticated with a PIN code.
  • Online Order: Flags transactions made over the internet, which are typically more prone to fraud.

Why It Matters

Credit card fraud is not just an inconvenience—it's a global issue that results in over $30 billion in losses annually. For consumers, it means lost money, frozen accounts, and hours spent recovering from unauthorized charges. For businesses and banks, it damages trust and brings legal and regulatory challenges.

Our goal is to reduce this risk by using intelligent fraud detection models that identify suspicious transactions in real time. By looking at unusual patterns—like a sudden transaction from a different country, or a purchase much larger than the typical spend—we can flag potential fraud before it causes harm.

Fraud detection not only protects users, but also strengthens the financial ecosystem by reducing false positives and improving the customer experience. It’s a win for security and convenience.
